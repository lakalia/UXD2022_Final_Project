Introduction Research Section 3

In 2006, renowned Internet-based enterprise Amazon launched a worldwide streaming service, Amazon Unbox. Over 15 years later, after various name changes, design variations and techological advancements, Amazon Prime Video is a formidable contender for the title of best streaming service.

Over the course of 14 weeks, our team has carefully studied various topics of User Interface (UI) and User Experience (UI) design. During that baseline research, our team initiated an investigation into the current Amazon Prime Video user experience with the goal of evaluating UI/UX flaws and identifying various solutions to them. The following content of this page depicts the research and design journey that our team undertook.
